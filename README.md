## Student & Parent Performance Dashboard Using Excel

##  Project Objective
To analyze student performance and understand the influence of gender, school type, guardian, and parental education on study time, marks, activities, and overall academic outcomes.

## Dataset Used
<a href="https://github.com/kowsalyas56/student-and-parent-excel-1/blob/main/student%20data.xlsx"> Data Set</a>

## Questions 
- How do study time and travel time differ by gender?
- Which school performs better in G1, G2, and G3 marks?
- How do student activities vary by guardian?
- How does parental education differ by guardian type?
- What age group and school combination impacts performance?
Dashboard Interactions :<a href="https://github.com/kowsalyas56/student-and-parent-excel-1/blob/main/Screenshot%20(44).png">View Dashboard</a>

## Process
- Imported dataset into Power BI
- Cleaned data and corrected data types
- Created visuals: bar charts, donut charts, line charts
- Added slicers for age and school
- Combined student and parent data to understand relationships
- Designed dashboard layout and formatting

## Project Insight
- Female students spend more study time than males.
- GP school shows higher marks in G1 and G2 compared to MS.
- Students with mother as guardian show higher participation in freetime and school support activities.
- Mother has the highest recorded education level in most cases.
- Travel time is generally lower for female students, giving them more time to study.

## Final Conclusion
The dashboard clearly highlights how gender, guardian type, and school influence academic performance.
